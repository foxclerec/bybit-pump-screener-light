Overview

Pump Alerts is a free, open-source application that runs entirely on your device. It does not collect personal data — no telemetry, no analytics, no cookies, no accounts.

Data the App Processes

The App fetches public market data from cryptocurrency exchange APIs (prices, trading volumes, candlestick data). This data is processed locally on your device and is not sent to any server controlled by the project maintainers.

All application data (signals, settings, detection rules) is stored in a local SQLite database on your device. We have no access to this data.

Data We May Receive From You

Support messages: If you contact us via email for support or bug reports, we receive your message and contact details in order to reply. We do not sell or rent personal information.

Third-Party Services

The App fetches public market data directly from exchange APIs. Your IP address is visible to these exchanges when the App makes API requests. Your use of these services is subject to their own terms and privacy policies.

Donations are processed by NOWPayments, a third-party payment gateway. If you choose to donate, your transaction is handled entirely by NOWPayments under their own terms and privacy policy. We do not receive or store any personal data from your transaction.

How We Use Information

  • To reply to support requests and bug reports.
  • To comply with applicable legal obligations.

Data Retention

Support messages are kept only as long as needed to handle your request. You can ask us to delete your messages after a case is closed.

Security

The App runs locally and does not transmit your data to external servers. You are responsible for securing your device and any local files or databases created by the App.

Your Rights

Depending on your location (e.g., EU/EEA/UK GDPR, California CCPA/CPRA), you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your data, and to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority. To make a request, contact us via email.

Children

The App is intended for adults (18+). We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

Changes

If we change this policy, we will update the text on this page. Continued use of the App after changes means you accept the updated policy.
