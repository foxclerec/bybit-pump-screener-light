This Risk Disclosure supplements our Risk Disclaimer and Terms of Service. It describes specific risks associated with using Pump Alerts. You should read this document in its entirety before using the App.

1. Cryptocurrency Market Risks

1.1 Extreme Volatility

Cryptocurrency prices can experience rapid and severe fluctuations. It is not uncommon for a token to gain or lose 50% or more of its value within a single day, and in some cases within minutes. Such volatility can result in substantial or total loss of your capital.

1.2 Liquidity Risk

Many altcoins and newly listed tokens have very low trading volumes. Low liquidity means you may not be able to buy or sell at your desired price, and large orders may move the market significantly against you. In extreme cases, it may be impossible to exit a position at any reasonable price.

1.3 Delisting & Discontinuation

Exchanges may delist tokens at any time, with little or no advance notice. Delisted tokens may lose most or all of their value and become extremely difficult to sell. The App cannot predict or warn about future delistings.

1.4 Market Manipulation

Cryptocurrency markets are largely unregulated and susceptible to various forms of manipulation, including pump-and-dump schemes, wash trading, and spoofing. Signals generated by the App may detect price movements caused by manipulation. Acting on such signals carries elevated risk, as manipulated prices typically reverse sharply once the manipulation ends.

1.5 No Investor Protection

Unlike traditional financial markets, cryptocurrency trading is generally not covered by investor protection schemes, deposit insurance (such as FDIC or FSCS), or compensation funds. If an exchange fails, is hacked, or becomes insolvent, you may have no recourse to recover your funds.

1.6 Continuous Trading

Cryptocurrency markets operate 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, with no scheduled market closures, circuit breakers, or trading halts. Significant price movements can occur at any time, including while you are asleep or otherwise unavailable.

2. Software & Technical Risks

2.1 Alert Accuracy & Timeliness

Alerts generated by the App may be delayed, missed entirely, or contain inaccurate data. Causes include but are not limited to: software bugs, network latency, API rate limiting, exchange-side data delays, system resource constraints, and unexpected interactions between software components. Never rely solely on this App for time-sensitive trading decisions.

2.2 Data Integrity

Market data displayed by the App is sourced from third-party exchange APIs. This data may be incomplete, delayed, or incorrect. The App performs no independent verification of data accuracy. Discrepancies between data shown in the App and actual market conditions may exist at any time.

2.3 Availability & Uptime

The App is provided without any uptime or availability guarantees. It may become temporarily or permanently unavailable due to bugs, crashes, system failures, dependency updates, or other technical issues. No compensation or notification is guaranteed in the event of downtime.

2.4 Local Execution Risks

The App runs on your local device. Its performance and reliability depend on your hardware, operating system, network connection, antivirus software, and system configuration. Issues in your local environment may cause the App to malfunction or produce unreliable results.

3. Detection Methodology Risks

3.1 Not Predictive

The pump detection algorithm analyzes recent historical price data to identify price surges exceeding user-defined thresholds over specific lookback periods. This is a backward-looking, pattern-matching approach. It does not predict, forecast, or guarantee future price direction. A detected "pump" may immediately reverse, continue, consolidate, or be entirely meaningless in the broader market context.

3.2 False Signals

The algorithm may produce false positives (alerting on noise, low-volume spikes, or manipulation) and false negatives (missing genuine pump events due to timing, configuration, or data gaps). No detection system is perfect, and this one is no exception.

3.3 Configuration Sensitivity

Detection results depend heavily on user-configured parameters (lookback windows, percentage thresholds, volume filters, etc.). Misconfigured settings may produce excessive false alerts or miss genuine events. The App provides no guidance on optimal configuration for any particular trading strategy.

3.4 No Validation or Certification

The detection methodology used by the App has not been peer-reviewed, independently audited, back-tested on a statistically significant dataset, or certified by any regulatory body. It is an experimental, best-effort implementation.

4. Third-Party Dependency Risks

4.2 Exchange Terms & Restrictions

Your use of market data obtained through the App is subject to the respective exchange's terms of service. Some exchanges prohibit certain uses of their data or restrict access from specific jurisdictions. You are responsible for complying with all applicable exchange terms.

4.3 No Affiliation

5. Regulatory & Compliance Risks

5.1 Evolving Regulations

Cryptocurrency regulation is evolving rapidly across jurisdictions. New laws, enforcement actions, or regulatory guidance may affect the legality of cryptocurrency trading, the availability of exchanges, and the classification of digital assets in your jurisdiction at any time.

5.2 Jurisdictional Restrictions

Some exchanges monitored by the App operate under restrictions that prohibit users from certain countries or regions. You are solely responsible for verifying that your use of the App and any associated trading activities comply with the laws and regulations of your jurisdiction.
